Introduction

Data collection

We have collected data from IMDB which is a database of all the cinematographic records in the entire world. We have combined this database with the TMDB API which allows us to obtain information about the revenue and budget of each movie and some information about Oscars Awards to analyze that is useful to analyze the success of movies. The TMDB API could only provide us information about the revenue and budget of around ten thousand movies. For this research question, we narrowed down to only movies and we excluded tv series, documentaries, tv shows, etc.

About Us

We, a collective of computer science scholars currently in our fifth semester pursuing our Bachelor's degree at Christian-Albrechts-Universität Kiel, harbor a profound interest in comprehending the contemporary landscape of global movie productions. Driven by this curiosity, we have embarked upon a rigorous inquiry aimed at elucidating the evolution of various critical factors associated with cinematic endeavors since the year 1915.

To achieve this noble pursuit, we have formulated the following research questions as guiding beacons: "How has the average budget, revenue, genre distribution, and runtime of movies evolved over the years?" Through meticulous analysis and diligent study, we aspire to unravel the intricate tapestry of trends and transformations that have shaped the cinematic realm throughout its storied history.

Our endeavor stands as a testament to our unwavering dedication to scholarly inquiry and intellectual exploration. It is our sincere hope that our research endeavors not only captivate your interest but also provide valuable insights into the dynamic landscape of contemporary movie production.
